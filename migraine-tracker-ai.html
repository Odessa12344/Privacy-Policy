Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Migraine Tracker AI – Headache Diary & Trigger Analyzer mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the “Application”) developed and published by Sergey Medvedev (hereinafter referred to as the “Developer”, “Service Provider”, “we”, “us”, or “our”).

The Application is distributed through Google Play as a Freemium service and is provided “AS IS” without warranties of any kind.

Information We Collect and How We Use It

The Application is a personal migraine and headache tracking tool designed to help users monitor symptoms and identify possible triggers. Users may voluntarily enter health-related information into the Application, including but not limited to:

  • Date and time of headache episodes
  • Pain level (0–10) and Duration of headaches
  • Location of pain
  • Associated symptoms (e.g., nausea, sensitivity to light or sound, aura)
  • Possible triggers (e.g., stress, sleep patterns, diet, weather)
  • Personal notes
Important: This information (User Health Data) is stored locally on the user’s device only using an Android Room database. The Developer does not collect, store, transmit, or access this User Health Data on external servers.

Artificial Intelligence Features

The Application may include optional AI-powered analysis features designed to help users identify patterns or potential migraine triggers. When a user voluntarily initiates an AI analysis request:

  • Only the minimum necessary anonymized excerpts of migraine logs may be temporarily transmitted to an AI service provider (such as Google AI services like Gemini).
  • The transmitted data does not include personal identifiers.
  • The Developer does not store or retain this data after the analysis is completed.
  • AI providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application automatically collects limited non-personally identifiable technical information to improve performance and reliability. This may include:

  • Device manufacturer and model, and Operating system version
  • Application version and build number
  • Crash reports and diagnostic information
  • General app usage events (e.g., app opens, screen views)

This information is collected through services provided by Google, including: Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Remote Config.

Advertising

The Application may display advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect limited information such as:

  • Advertising Identifier (Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Device information and Ad interaction events

You can disable personalized advertising in your device settings.

App Permissions

The Application may request the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: required for Advertising (Google AdMob), Analytics (Firebase), Crash reporting, and Optional AI analysis features.

These permissions are used only for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The Application may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Firebase Analytics (Usage analytics)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Crash reporting)
  • Firebase Remote Config (Feature configuration)
  • Google Play Billing (Subscriptions and in-app purchases)

These services may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Retention

User Health Data entered in the Application remains stored locally on the user’s device until:

  • The user deletes it manually
  • The user clears application data
  • The user uninstalls the Application

Automatically collected analytics or advertising data may be retained by Google services according to their own retention policies.

Opt-Out and Data Deletion

Users can stop all data collection by: Uninstalling the Application, resetting the Advertising ID in device settings, or disabling personalized ads in Android settings.

Children’s Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been provided, we will take steps to delete it.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data processed by the Application. User Health Data remains stored locally on the device and is protected by Android system security mechanisms. Data transmitted to third-party services is encrypted in transit according to their security standards.
