Migraine Tracker AI - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Migraine Tracker AI – Headache Diary & Trigger Analyzer mobile application (the “Application”) developed by Sergey Medvedev (“Developer”, “we”, “us”).

The Application is distributed via Google Play as a Freemium service and is provided “AS IS”.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

The Application is a personal tracking tool. Users may voluntarily enter health-related information, including:

  • Date and time of headache episodes
  • Pain level (0–10) and duration
  • Location of pain and symptoms (nausea, aura, etc.)
  • Possible triggers (stress, diet, weather)
Storage: This information (User Health Data) is stored locally on the user’s device only using an Android Room database. The Developer does not access or store this data on external servers.

2. Artificial Intelligence Features

If you use our optional AI analysis features:

  • Only anonymized excerpts of logs are temporarily sent to AI providers (e.g., Google Gemini).
  • Transmitted data does not include personal identifiers.
  • Data is not retained by the Developer after analysis.

3. Automatically Collected Information

To improve reliability, we collect non-identifiable technical data via Google Firebase:

  • Device model and OS version
  • Crash reports and diagnostic info
  • General usage events (e.g., app opens)

4. Advertising

The Application uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location (IP-based)
  • Ad interaction events

You can disable personalized ads in your Android device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following services, which process data under their own policies:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)
  • Google Play Billing (In-app purchases)

View Google's Privacy Policy

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. Since User Health Data is stored locally, it is protected by the Android system's built-in security mechanisms.
