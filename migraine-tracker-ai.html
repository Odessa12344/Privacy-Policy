This Privacy Policy applies to the Migraine Tracker AI mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the “Application”) developed by Sergey Medvedev (“Developer”, “we”, “us”, or “our”).

1. Information Collection and Health Data

The Application is designed to track migraine episodes and symptoms. You may voluntarily enter Sensitive Personal Information, including:

  • Headache date, time, duration, and pain severity.
  • Pain location and associated symptoms (nausea, aura, etc.).
  • Potential triggers (diet, sleep, stress, weather).
  • Personal notes related to your condition.
Data Sovereignty: All health-related logs are stored locally on your device only (Android Room Database). We do not collect, transmit, or store this sensitive data on our own servers. You have full control over your data.

2. Artificial Intelligence (AI) Processing

The Application features an "AI Trigger Analyzer". When you use this feature:

  • Only anonymized excerpts of your logs (e.g., "Attack after 5h sleep") are sent to Google Gemini API for pattern recognition.
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is included in these requests.
  • This data is processed temporarily and is not retained by the Developer.

3. Automatically Collected Data

To ensure app stability and improve user experience, we use third-party tools that collect non-identifiable technical data:

  • Firebase Crashlytics & Analytics: Device info, crash logs, and basic usage patterns.
  • Google AdMob: Advertising ID for displaying relevant ads (you can opt-out in Android settings).
  • Google Play Billing: To process subscriptions safely. We do not see your credit card details.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Your health data stays on your phone until you:

  • Manually delete entries in the app.
  • Clear the Application's cache/data in Android settings.
  • Uninstall the Application.

5. Children’s Privacy

The Application is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. If we discover such data was provided, we will delete it immediately from local storage instructions.

6. Security

We rely on standard Android security protocols to protect data stored on your device. Since we do not use cloud storage for your health logs, the security of your data largely depends on your device's security (PIN, biometrics).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy. Changes are effective immediately upon posting to this page. Continued use of the Application signifies your consent to the updated terms.
