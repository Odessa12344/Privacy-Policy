Freebie Games & Drops

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Freebie Games & Drops collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1. Overview

Freebie Games & Drops helps users discover free games, free-to-play titles, giveaways, subscription rewards, and Twitch Drops across different platforms and stores.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect limited information necessary to operate the app, including:

  • Technical information such as device type, app version, operating system, and language settings.
  • Push notification tokens used to send alerts about new offers and drops.
  • Advertising-related data provided by Google AdMob to display rewarded ads.
  • Basic usage information needed to improve app stability and performance.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as passwords, payment data, or private messages.

3. How We Use Information

  • To load and display live offers, drops, and promotions.
  • To send push notifications about new free games, drops, and rewards.
  • To show rewarded ads before certain claim or open actions.
  • To maintain app performance, reliability, and security.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services, including:

  • Google Firebase Cloud Messaging for push notifications.
  • Google AdMob for rewarded advertising.
  • External gaming stores and offer pages opened through links inside the app.

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

5. External Links

Freebie Games & Drops may redirect you to third-party websites, store pages, or offer pages such as Steam, Epic Games, GOG, Xbox, Twitch, Google Play, and others. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share limited technical data when required to operate app features such as notifications, ads, analytics, or external store links.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information used by the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Children’s Privacy

Free Games & Drops is not directed specifically to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the developer contact information listed in Google Play.
